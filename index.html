Legal Wiz

As a finalist team of Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2020, we had developed this website using front end technologies like HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Bootstrap, etc. and for backend, we have used Python-Django with integrated SQLite3 database.

The Secretary/Admin and a Nodal Officer are only authorized persons to access this important information of the organization. The Nodal Officer of an organization can access and manage details like case invoices, next upcoming hearings, number of cases on organization, and case status along with the advocate list.

  • Key Features:
    • Lots of paperwork of the nodal officer is reduced.
    • User-friendly interface.
    • One can send SMS for giving reminders of upcoming hearings.
    • Can view the previous history of closed cases and judgment details.
    • Secure as only two higher authorities have access, no other employees are allowed to modify the organization's important details.
